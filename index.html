How we’re making a difference At Flash Forward, we believe that light is more than just illumination—it’s a tool for learning, growth, and security. Our mission is to provide solar-powered flashlights to children in off-grid communities, ensuring they have the light they need to study, walk safely at night, and build a brighter future.

The secret to happiness lies in helping others. Never underestimate the difference YOU can make in the lives of the poor, the abused and the helpless.
No electricity means children struggle to do homework after sunset, limiting their education and reinforcing the cycle of poverty. Without reliable light, opportunities for learning, growth, and a brighter future remain out of reach.
