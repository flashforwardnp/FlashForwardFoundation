How we’re making a difference

At Flash Forward, we believe that light is more than just illumination—it’s a tool for learning, growth, and security. Our mission is to provide solar-powered flashlights to children in off-grid communities, ensuring they have the light they need to study, walk safely at night, and build a brighter future.

The secret to happiness lies in helping others. Never underestimate the difference YOU can make in the lives of the poor, the abused, and the helpless.

No electricity means children struggle to do homework after sunset, limiting their education and reinforcing the cycle of poverty.


With reliable light, opportunities for learning, growth, and a brighter future become achievable for children in need.

Partnerships

At Flash Forward, we believe that collaboration strengthens impact. That’s why we’re partnering with the Sammamish High School Builders Club, an engineering-focused student organization dedicated to hands-on innovation. This collaboration also embodies our mission of uniting local communities to drive global change. Together, we’re building a brighter future—one light at a time.

Our Vision

A world where no child’s future is limited by darkness. We envision a future where every child, regardless of location or background, has access to safe, sustainable lighting. By leveraging solar technology, we’re creating long-term impact—one flashlight at a time.
