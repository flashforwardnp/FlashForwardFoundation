Our Story

Flash Forward Foundation began with a simple observation: millions of children around the world struggle to study after sunset due to lack of reliable lighting. This challenge particularly affects communities without stable electricity access, limiting educational opportunities and perpetuating the cycle of poverty.

Founded by passionate students across 3 schools, our initiative started as a local project but quickly grew into a movement. We recognized that by providing solar-powered flashlights, we could create immediate, tangible impact while promoting sustainable solutions.
